Our Mission

Here at Canine Conundrums, we understand that your animal companions are more than just pets, they are members of your family. That is why it is our goal to help your animals acheive their fullest potential, both physically and spiritually, while treating them with the utmost respect and care they merit.

Many people do not realize the immense physical and psychological stresses that the average house companions deal with on a daily basis or that leaving these ailments untreated can create long lasting damage to your animal children. Our staff here at Canine Conundrums have done extensive research in the field of animal healthcare and offer a multitude of services to meet our patients' needs, ranging from massage therapy to intensive psychic readings.

If your animal companion is suffering or if they simply need some time away for rest and rejuvenation, please call us today for a free consultation. We look forward to assisting your family members find the balance in their lives they rightfully deserve.
